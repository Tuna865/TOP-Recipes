Chili Mac

Ingredients

  • macaroni noodles
  • ground beef
  • paprika
  • salt & pepper
  • chili powder
  • red onion
  • jalapeno pepper
  • can of kidney beans
  • can of diced tomatoes
  • cilantro
  • cheddar cheese
  • heavy cream
  • flour

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet
  2. Add spices
  3. Drain any grease from beef
  4. add beans, onion, and tomatoes to beef
  5. Make cheese sauce using flour, cheddar, and cream
  6. Add cheese sauce to beef mixture
  7. Cook macaroni noodles
  8. Strain water from macaroni noodles & add to beef
  9. Dice jalapeno for garnish
  10. Enjoy!
