Chili Mac

Ingredients

  • macaroni noodles
  • ground beef
  • red onion
  • jalapeno pepper
  • cilantro
  • cheddar cheese
  • heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Dice pork shoulder into 1 inch cubes
  2. Coat pork cubes in olive oil and then apply spices
  3. Add pork cubes to pressure cooker and coat with OJ
  4. Cook on high pressure for 75min
  5. Remove from pressure cooker and broil for 5min
  6. Serve with tortillas, toppings, and sides!
