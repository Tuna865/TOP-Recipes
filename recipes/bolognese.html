Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • salt
  • spaghetti
  • pasta sauce
  • ground beef
  • italian spices
  • cayenne pepper
  • cheddar cheese
  • parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook beef and drain any fat
  3. Add spices and pasta sauce to beef
  4. Simmer bolognese sauce for 30 min to 1 hour
  5. Add cheddar cheese to sauce and mix until melted
  6. Cook spaghetti noodles
  7. Once noodles are al dente, serve with bolognese sauce
  8. Enjoy!
